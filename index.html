About

My name is Margarida, which stands for a tiny simple flower that grows pretty much everywhere. As a post-Instagram young woman, I was always haunted by the terrible absence of an ultimate passion in my life. I kept looking for that thing I could earn my life with and “not needing to work another day”, as clichés go. I studied Communication Sciences because I like to tell stories, in many ways as possible.

By the time I graduated, the lack of THE passion was almost choking me. So, as any other standard human being would do, I did nothing. I didn’t plan ahead, got an internship nor enrol in further studies. I just calmly thought on going to the beach on the following week.

I ended up in Australia for three months and that event could pretty much sum my lifestyle – at least I hope it does! I haven’t got one single passion, I’m just curious. I love to learn new things, from farming to programming, and I have the most fun telling people about what I learn, see and experience.
