Laughing Kookaburra

Laughing Kookaburras may seem they're laughing, but are actually marking their territory.

  • Scientific Name: Dacelo novaeguineae
  • Average Length: 39 to 45 cm
  • Average Lifespan: Up to 20 years
  • Habitat: Eucalypt forests, open woodlands, edges of plains in Eastern Australia

Known for their "laughing" call, the laughing kookaburra is a monogamous, territorial bird that nests in tree holes. They have a beak that can reach 10 cm in order to snacth a wide variety of invertebrate and vertebrate animals, including small snakes!
